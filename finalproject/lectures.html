Dr. Lind in Action: Global Lectures & Hands-On Training

Dr. Stacy Doyle Lind has lectured in over 98 countries and 162 dental schools, sharing his expertise in advanced dentistry, implantology, prosthodontics, and digital workflows. His presentations blend clinical precision with engaging instruction, making complex topics approachable and actionable for dental professionals around the world.

Lecture Topics

Cosmetic Dentistry

Dr. Lind presents advanced techniques in composite restorations using state-of-the-art polymer chemistry. This lecture explores how to achieve highly esthetic, durable results for natural-looking smiles that meet both functional and cosmetic demands. Emphasis is placed on patient satisfaction and long-term success.

Soft Tissue Grafting

Gain hands-on experience with modern soft tissue grafting techniques designed to restore gum health and esthetics. This lecture focuses on root coverage procedures, ridge preservation, and tissue regeneration to improve both functional outcomes and smile harmony.

Prosthodontics and Full Mouth Comprehensive Treatment

Discover a systematic approach to full mouth rehabilitation using advanced prosthodontic techniques. Dr. Lind walks through crown and bridge planning, provisional temporization, and smile design for transformative, life-changing patient outcomes.

Implantology & Surgical Protocols

Master guided implant placement, bone grafting, and restorative workflows using systems like Nobel and BioHorizons. This lecture demystifies surgical protocols and streamlines the integration of implants into everyday practice.

Bio-Esthetics & Oriognathic Function

Explore the connection between neuromuscular harmony and esthetic design. This lecture covers joint-based occlusal theory and functional smile development to treat TMJ disorders and improve long-term patient comfort and function.

CAD/CAM and Digital Workflows

Step into the digital age of dentistry with CAD/CAM solutions like CEREC and 3D printing. Learn how to increase efficiency and accuracy in restorative cases with fully integrated digital workflows.

Sinus lifts and bone augmentation

Elevate your surgical skill set with techniques for sinus lift procedures and ridge augmentation. Learn lateral and crestal approaches to enhance bone volume and improve implant outcomes in complex cases.

Practice Management & Growth

Unlock the tools to grow a successful, patient-centered dental practice. This lecture highlights efficient scheduling systems, treatment planning strategies, and proven communication methods that boost case acceptance and profitability.
